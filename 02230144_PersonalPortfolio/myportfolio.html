Projects

Bank Management System

The Bank Management System is a project I worked on during second semesters, along with my group members. We developed a simple banking application using Java. This system aims to streamline the management of bank operations by automating tasks such as account creation, fund transfers, balance inquiries, and transaction history tracking.

Personal Expense Tracker

The Personal Expense Tracker is another project I worked on with my group members. This application helps individuals track and manage their daily expenses. It allows users to record their income, categorize their spending, and set budget goals. Since it is a database-driven project, we focused heavily on collecting and organizing user data efficiently. By storing and analyzing the data, the app provides users with an overview of their financial habits, helping them make more informed decisions about their spending, save money, and stay within their budget.

College Website

In my second year, my group and I worked on remaking the CST College website as part of our project. We used Figma to design a prototype and carefully studied the existing college website. Our goal was to recreate a similar website, but with improvements in design and functionality. By doing so, we gained hands-on experience in web design and development while ensuring the website would meet the needs of both students and faculty.
